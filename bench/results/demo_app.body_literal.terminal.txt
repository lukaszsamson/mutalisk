## Recommendation
Keep body_literal opt-in for v1.9. 83% kill rate on a small
fixture is encouraging but not yet conclusive — the guards.ex
survivor is a tell that some real-world bodies will produce
equivalent mutants the suite can't distinguish (e.g., a guarded
function whose only test case exercises the truthy clause).
The 28% wall-clock cost is acceptable for users who opt in,
unacceptable as a default tax. Re-evaluate flipping default-on
after M24's planned OSS-target validation completes.
